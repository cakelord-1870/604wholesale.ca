Outdoor Furniture

At 604Wholesale all of our outdoor patio furniture is made with a powder-coated resin wicker and is guaranteed not to fade or crack, and is backed by our 5 YEAR WARRANTY.  All of our products are constructed with industrial-grade aluminum framing, which is gauranteed not to rust and is also backed by our 5 YEAR WARRANTY!  Additionally, all of our cushions are zippered encased, making the covers easy to remove and machine wash. Furthermore, since all of our covers are removable, you can even order brand new covers in any colour to change the look of your set!

What propels us above and beyond our competitors is not only our top quality product, but the fact that you can order ANY MODEL in various wicker and cushion colors.  We can also customize individual items, so if you have a deck with a specific size, we can construct something that works perfectly in your space.
