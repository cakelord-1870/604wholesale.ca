Sectionals

Sofas and sectionals at 604wholesale are made with the highest quality materials! They are all made with 100% genuine Italian leather, and are available in a 1.8mm or 2.1 mm thickness. The frame is constructed with Canadian Birch wood (not MDF) like some other companies.

Each item comes with a 2 year warranty which covers the leather or fabric, the stitching and the frame.  We do not carry or have access to bonded leather however each item is available in various array of velvet, microfiber or polyester fabrics.  Lead times on items are roughly 6 weeks if not in stock.
